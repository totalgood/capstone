finnegan

by Cole Howard

A custom neural net with a serious nod to I Am Trask for recoginzing handwritten digits. The neural net is pre-trained on the MNIST dataset (run on an AWS instance). And the user input is rescaled and tested.

To Get Started

Navigate to: Finnegan

The baseline architecture for the net is at Github

To adjust hyper-parameters or choose dataset: Open net_launch.py and adjust as needed at the bottom of the file. The dataset can be switched by commenting out the appropriate line at the bottom and uncommneting the other. Or simply:

from Network import network

And feed it the appropriate parameters.
